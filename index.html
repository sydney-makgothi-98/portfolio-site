About Me

I'm currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Aerospace at Wits University. Alongside my studies, I work as an experienced tutor at TeachMe2, where I’ve helped students master subjects such as Mechanics of Materials, Thermofluids, Mathematics, and Software Development.

My technical expertise includes Python, JavaScript, C++, MATLAB, HTML, and CSS, which I leverage to develop Django-projects such as CRM solutions for small to medium enterprises. I am certified in applied calculus, which I earned from Harvard. I’m passionate about software development, particularly in creating efficient and scalable web applications.

Outside of academics and coding, I’m a huge anime fan—Hunter x Hunter and Fairy Tail are two of my all-time favorites. When it comes to music, I gravitate toward alternative hip-hop, with artists like MIKE, Earl Sweatshirt, and Nion-Tay ranking among my top influences.

Outside of academics and coding, I’m a big fan of Formula 1, currently rooting for Lewis Hamilton, and I follow basketball, supporting the Indiana Pacers. In my free time, I work as a freelance video editor—you can check out one of my projects [HERE](link to YT video).
